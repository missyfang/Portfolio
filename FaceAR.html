FACE AR

FaceAR is an augmented reality app developed using Unity, designed to assist individuals with facial paralysis who are required to perform facial exercises as part of their rehabilitation. The app uses face tracking technology to guide users through accurate facial exercise sequences and interactive games. It also provides instant feedback on the user's performance, ensuring proper muscle activation. My responsibilities on the project included designing an interface that enables easy access to ARkit's face-tracking tools for our team developers, and creating interactive games to enhance the user's experience while performing facial exercises.
