CarePack

CarePack is a mobile app where users can create virtual care packages. These packages can include photos, videos, embedded links, stickers, and drawings. Users also get to design the outside of their package and write a message that recipients will read before opening the package. Our design is meant to enforce a more mindful and substantive form of communication to ensure the virtual interactions between our users and their long-distance friends are meaningful.
