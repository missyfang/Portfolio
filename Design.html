Blue Sky Initiative

As part of the University of Michigan's Blue Sky Initiative, I actively contributed to various projects: My involvement included the development of an educational video game, where I played a role in crafting engaging game art. Additionally, I created a range of 3D and 2D graphics using Blender and Adobe software to support different research endeavors. Notably, one of my 3D renders was chosen for the cover of an ACS Nano publication after our team's submission. Alongside this, I specialized in developing 3D graphics for journal cover submissions and crafting 2D scientific graphics aimed at elucidating complex microbiology concepts for high school students.

Check out my 2D scientific graphics in this student work packet!
