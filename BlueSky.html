The Blue Sky Initiative

Technical Research Assistant, MAY 2021 - MAY 2022

As a developer and designer, my roles on this project included game development and graphic design. In terms of game development, I was responsible for designing game art and implementing the game's user interface features. After the completion of the game, I was retained by the team as a graphic artist. In this role, I created a variety of 2D and 3D graphics for research projects and assisted educators in creating a student work packet for high school biology students.
