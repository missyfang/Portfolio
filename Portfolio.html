Rock Climber

The inspiration for this game came from the movement and techniques used in rock climbing. It is a vertical scrolling puzzle platform game where players must navigate a scrolling rock wall to find a valid route while avoiding bad holds and falling rocks. In developing the game, I focused on creating an intuitive experience that requires no prior knowledge of rock climbing or reading text to understand the game rules. Instead, I guide players through iterative levels that introduce new mechanics before testing their new skills in subsequent, faster and more challenging levels.

Legend of Zelda

In this recreation of Legend of Zelda level 1 dungeon, My contributions include implementing enemies, a health system, keyboard input, and many of the mechanisms of the custom level. The unique mechanic of the custom level is a mirrored clone of Link, whose horizontal movement mirrors the player's movements, adding a new challenge to puzzle-solving. Players must coordinate both Link and the clone's movements and manipulate the environment to progress.
